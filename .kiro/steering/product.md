# Product Overview

**Leia Sabores** is a high-performance e-commerce platform specializing in custom cake toppers and artisanal sweets. The platform is built for production with a focus on mobile-first design and global performance.

## Key Features

- **Product Focus**: Custom cake toppers ("Topos de Bolo") and artisanal sweets
- **Payment Integration**: Stripe checkout with Apple Pay, Google Pay, MBWay, and card payments
- **Performance**: Built on Cloudflare's edge infrastructure for global performance
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design optimized for mobile shopping experience
- **PWA Ready**: Progressive Web App with manifest and service worker

## Target Market

Portuguese-speaking customers looking for personalized cake decorations and artisanal sweets, with emphasis on custom orders and premium quality products.

## Business Model

E-commerce with direct sales, custom product personalization, and integrated payment processing with automatic order fulfillment workflows.
